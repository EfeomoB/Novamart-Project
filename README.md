# 🛒 NovaMart Insights Project

## 📖 Overview
**NovaMart Insights** is a comprehensive business intelligence and analytics project designed to uncover key performance trends, improve profitability, and optimize operations for NovaMart — an online subscription-based retail platform.  

Through a combination of **SQL data analysis**, **Power BI dashboards**, and **strategic insights**, this project transforms raw business data into actionable intelligence for decision-makers.

---

## 🚩 Business Challenges
The project addressed several pressing issues faced by NovaMart:
- ❌ **Unclear bundle profitability** — difficulty determining which product bundles generate the most value  
- ❌ **Lack of customer segmentation** — limited visibility into customer behavior and loyalty tiers  
- ❌ **Payment inconsistencies** — irregular patterns across payment methods and cycles  
- ❌ **Scattered feedback data** — no unified view of customer satisfaction and support performance  
- ❌ **No standard KPIs** — inconsistent measurement of business performance  

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives
1. Establish a **data-driven decision-making framework**
2. Define and track **standardized KPIs** for all key operations  
3. Develop **SQL-based insights** for revenue, customer behavior, and payments  
4. Design **interactive Power BI dashboards** for executive reporting  
5. Provide **strategic recommendations** to improve growth, efficiency, and customer experience  

---

## 🧠 Data & Methodology
The analytical process followed a structured data pipeline:
1. **Data Extraction & Cleaning:**  
   Aggregated data from multiple sources (sales, customers, payments, support tickets).
2. **SQL Analysis:**  
   Wrote optimized SQL queries to calculate KPIs, revenue trends, and city-level performance.
3. **Visualization:**  
   Built **Power BI dashboards** for real-time monitoring and performance comparison.
4. **Insight Generation:**  
   Translated quantitative findings into business insights and actionable recommendations.

---

## 📊 Key KPIs Defined
| KPI | Description |
|-----|--------------|
| **Monthly Revenue Growth** | Tracks revenue increase across time periods |
| **Bundle Profitability** | Measures contribution margins by product bundle |
| **Customer Segmentation (Loyalty Tiers)** | Classifies users by spend and retention |
| **Payment Success Rate** | Evaluates consistency and reliability of transactions |
| **Ticket Resolution & Ratings** | Correlates customer feedback with support efficiency |

---

## 💡 SQL Insights (Sample Findings)
- 📈 Certain **medium-sized bundles** contributed **35% higher profit margins** than smaller ones.  
- 💳 **Auto-pay customers** had the **lowest payment failure rate (under 2%)**.  
- 🏙️ **Top 5 cities** generated nearly **60% of total subscription volume**.  
- ⭐ **High loyalty-tier customers** accounted for **45% of revenue** despite being only 20% of the customer base.  
- 💬 **Ticket ratings** improved by **18%** when issues were resolved within 24 hours.
